## Good to Know

**It remembers things.** Your AI keeps notes about your preferences, important details, and past conversations. The more you use it, the more helpful it becomes.

**It checks in.** A few times a day, your AI will proactively check your email and let you know if anything important comes in. You don't have to ask.

**It's private.** Everything stays on your Mac Mini. Your conversations, your emails, your data â€” none of it goes to us or anyone else. The only external service is Anthropic (the AI brain), and they don't store your conversations.

**It's always on.** The Mac Mini runs 24/7. Even if the power goes out, it'll come back online automatically when power returns.

**It's not perfect.** AI is incredibly capable but it can make mistakes. Always double-check important information, especially numbers, dates, and legal/medical advice.

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific.** "Find me a plumber" is good. "Find me a licensed plumber in [city] with good reviews who does weekend appointments" is better.

2. **Give context.** "Write an email" is vague. "Write a professional email to my client John about pushing the deadline back one week" gets great results.

3. **Teach it about you.** Tell it things like "I prefer morning appointments" or "I'm vegetarian" â€” it'll remember and factor that in next time.

4. **Just ask.** If you're not sure whether it can do something, just ask. The worst it'll say is "I can't do that."
